About Fluorescent Fluency

Fluorescent Fluency was not built to teach people a language. Rather, it was built to help people find resources that help them address their own specific needs. Fluorescent Fluency was made to help language learners to navigate the seemingly endless amount of resources out there. There are so many different books and websites that it is hard to know what's quality material. We aim to help clear the water, to provide a clean and accessible database for all of your language learning needs.

You may be thinking, what's with the name? Our lightbulb image symbolizes out goal of shedding light on language learning. It is also a nod to the classic idea of a a lightbulb symbolizing a sudden idea or burst of inspiration.

Even though some resources maybe be placed in only one category, we encourage you to explore on your own. There are hidden gems everywhere.
