Sinosplice Website

Resource Type: Website

This website goes well in depth about the fundamentals of pronouncing the different tones in Chinese. It gives many examples of tones and tone pairs to help improve speaking.

Badanamu Youtube Channel

Resouce Type: Youtube Channel

This Youtube Channel specializes in creating fun, catchy songs that are easy to sing along to. Sing along songs improve speed and pronunciation while learning a language. The songs include subtitles and often they are completely translated, which helps you to learn new vocabulary.

Off the Great Wall Youtube Channel

Resource Type: Youtube Channel

This upbeat, collaborative Youtube channel creates engaging videos. Combining informative learning videos, cultural videos, as well as everyday lifestyle videos, the Youtube channel has it all.

Yangyang Cheng Youtube Channel

Resource Type: Youtube Channel

This YouTube channel covers everything Chinese. Her videos range from how to pronounce a tone to how to say fractions. Her videos are to the point and clear and often offer visual aids.
