Señor Jordan

Resource Type: YouTube Channel (Videos)

Watch videos of Señor Jordan to learn the various Spanish grammatical rules, such as basic verb conjugation. They are fun, interactive videos and some even include songs that you won't be able to get out of your head!

StudySpanish

Resource Type: Website

Website that has a complete course on Spanish resource, taking you through the rules fundamentals of Spanish grammar. This website is more for those who like learning through reading instructions and examples.

121 Spanish

Resource Type: Website

A great tool to help you with your Spanish Grammar. It provides exercises from beginners to more advanced learners.

SpanishPod101

Resource Type: App and Website

This website allows you to listen to dialogue and then cover the grammar points and vocabulary. It is more on the serious side.

Duolingo

Resource Type: App and Website

Teaches you grammar, such as verb conjugations and pronouns through fun practice exercises. Keeps track of your progress as you become more fluent in Spanish.

SpanishDict

Resource Type: Website

A simple website that helps you conjugate verbs and translate phrases. It is a good tool to translate words and to find all of the various conjugations and tenses for verbs.

Conjuguemos

Resource Type: Website

A website created by language teachers, they have grammar lessons with worksheets and graded practice. The site is also good for vocabulary and listening.
